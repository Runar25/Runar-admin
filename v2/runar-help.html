HOW A READING WORKS

You come with a question — or without one. Both are welcome.

Before the stones speak, Rúnar asks a few things: your name, the area of life you are moving through, and what you are seeking. Not to judge. To listen more precisely.

Then you draw. One rune from twenty-five — plus the Blank, the rune of the unknown. What follows comes in two layers. The first is short and direct — the core of what the rune carries for you today. The second goes deeper, drawing on Norse mythology, the imagery of Iceland, and the thread between your drawn rune and your life rune.

The runes do not decide your path. They help you remember it.

RÚNAR'S VOICE

When you draw a rune in the collection, a sound plays — a pre-recorded tone of that rune's presence. Always the same. A note, not a reading.

When you use a reading card, Rúnar speaks your reading aloud. Not a recording. Generated in the moment, shaped around your specific words. Each time different. Each time his.

YOUR JOURNAL

Every reading you take is remembered. As a Rune Seeker, your last five readings wait in the journal — the rune drawn, the date, the area, the words spoken.

When you move to Rune Walker, the journal becomes a full chronicle. Every reading, going back to the beginning. Filterable by rune, area, and time.

LANGUAGE

Rúnar speaks two languages — English and Icelandic. These are not translations of each other. Each carries its own imagery, its own rhythm. Icelandic is older here. The runes were carved first in that tongue.

You can switch languages at any time from the menu. Your preference is remembered.

YOUR ACCOUNT & PRIVACY

Sign in with a magic link — no password needed — or with Google. Your email is the only thing we keep. Readings are stored privately. Only you can see them.

Your data is stored in Europe (Ireland). Iceland is a member of the European Economic Area. GDPR applies.

To delete your account and all your data, open the menu and choose Delete Account. Everything is erased immediately. Read our full Privacy Policy.
