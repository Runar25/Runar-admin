THE REASON WE HOLD IT

We process your data on the following legal grounds under GDPR Article 6:

Contract — to provide you with readings, journal, and account features you have requested.

Legitimate interest — to prevent abuse of the free reading allowance and to keep the service running reliably.

Consent — for optional features such as voice and language preferences.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making.

WHO ELSE SEES IT

We share only what is necessary, and only with these processors:

Supabase (database & authentication) — servers in EU West (Ireland). Your data never leaves the European Economic Area. supabase.com/privacy

Anthropic (AI readings) — your question and the rune drawn are sent to generate your reading. Anthropic does not store your data for training. anthropic.com/privacy

ElevenLabs (voice) — text of your reading is sent only when voice is active. elevenlabs.io/privacy

No data is sold. No data is shared with advertisers or data brokers. Ever.

YOUR RIGHTS

Under GDPR, these rights belong to you. They are not privileges — they are yours by law.

    COOKIES & LOCAL MEMORY

    Rúnar does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

    Your browser's local storage holds a small amount of information to keep the experience seamless: your chosen language, your name (if given), the count of readings this month, and a session token used to keep you signed in. This data never leaves your device unless explicitly synced to your account.

    You can clear this at any time through your browser settings, or by signing out of Rúnar.

    THE RIGHT TO BE FORGOTTEN

    You may delete your account at any time. Open the menu in Rúnar and choose Delete Account. This permanently removes your account, all readings, your journal, and all associated data from our systems.

    If you prefer, write to us at info@agndofa.is and we will do it for you within 30 days, as required by GDPR.

    Gift card codes you have redeemed remain in our records (without your personal data attached) for accounting purposes only.
